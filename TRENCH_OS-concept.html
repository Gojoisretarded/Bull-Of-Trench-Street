00 / THE NORTH STAR

Fun first. Simple enough to play with your thumbs.

Every decision serves one rule: is it fun, and is it easy to get? The simulator is the toy. If a feature needs a tutorial longer than a tweet, it’s too complicated — cut it or hide it.

THE PITCH

“One more trade.”

A degen fantasy you can open in a browser and *get* in ten seconds: you’re broke, the trenches are live, and you either read the chart or make the meme everyone else apes. Get rich, get famous, and never stop posting. That’s the game. Everything below is in service of that feeling.

03 / TWO WAYS TO PLAY

Read the chart, or be the reason it moves

Both hit the same wallet. Play one, play both, switch whenever. Whatever makes you more money is the right answer.

Path A

📈 The Trader

Live off other people’s coins. Spot the pump, dodge the rug, size up, take profit.

  • Buy low / sell high on the shared market
  • Read charts, momentum, and the feed for alpha
  • Skill = timing and nerve
OR
Path B

🎨 The Creator

Be the casino, not the gambler. Mint your own memecoin, hype it, and ride the wave you started.

  • Launch a coin on a bonding curve in seconds
  • Shill it on the feed to pull in real buyers
  • Skill = narrative and timing your exit
04 / THE SIGNATURE MECHANIC

Flex or larp — the thing his game doesn’t have

Your social rating decays if you go quiet, so you have to keep posting. Every post is a choice: show your real bag, or fake it. This is what makes the world yours.

● FLEX

Post your real PnL. Verified straight from your wallet, so it counts double — the trenches respect receipts. But you can only flex what you actually made.

caught real → clout moons, posts move markets harder

● LARP

Fake the gains. Post a screenshot of a bag you don’t have to farm clout you didn’t earn. Fast and free — until someone calls your bluff.

caught lying → rep nuked, followers bleed, name tarnished
05 / CLOUT = LEVERAGE

Money buys fame. Fame moves markets.

Social stats aren’t vanity — they’re a weapon, tied directly to how well you trade.

THE FLYWHEEL

Get rich → get famous → your word pumps coins → get richer.

The more you make, the more your social rating climbs. High enough, and a single post from you sends real buy pressure into a coin — including your own. But the rating decays when you go quiet, so you’re never done. Keep trading, keep posting, keep proving it. That tension is the engine of the whole social loop.

06 / ONE LIVE WORLD

Everyone in the same trenches, at the same time

The “way cooler” version, and worth the effort: a single shared, live market instead of a solo sandbox.

STRETCH GOAL

A real market, not a scripted one.

Coins live on bonding curves that respond to actual player buys and sells — so a pump is real demand, and a rug is real people bailing. Your shills move real prices; other players’ moves move yours. This is the single upgrade that turns a toy into a world. Ship solo-first if needed, wire the live market in right after.

07 / UNDER THE HOOD

What to build it with

Chosen so a solo dev can ship fast without repainting into a corner when players show up.

Client
React + TypeScript + Vite, Tailwind, framer-motion
The fake-desktop simulator UI. TypeScript is non-negotiable once real money-state exists.
State
Zustand (UI / windows) · TanStack Query (server data)
Zustand runs the desktop shell; Query owns anything the server is source-of-truth for.
Realtime
WebSockets — live market, feed, PnL posts, clout updates
One shared world needs a live pipe. Powers the flex/larp feed and moving prices.
Backend
Node + Fastify, REST for player “intents” (buy, mint, post)
Fast and schema-first — validate every action at the door. Client asks, server decides.
Game loop
Tick worker — owns the market, moves prices, resolves curves
The only thing allowed to move a price. Keeps the sim consistent for everyone.
Data
Postgres (wallets, coins, posts, clout) · Redis (live prices, leaderboards, anti-spam)
Postgres is the ledger of record; Redis is speed, ranks, and rate limits.
NPCs
Claude API — server-side, per-character personalities
claude-haiku-4-5 for background chatter, claude-sonnet-5 for story beats. Never from the client.
Auth
Real accounts day one + the frictionless recovery-code save
Supabase Auth or Lucia. Don’t bolt accounts on later like the original had to.
08 / WHY YOU CAN CHANGE ANYTHING

One engine. The lore is just content on top.

This is the important bit: characters, coins, story, even the whole theme are swappable content. The engine never changes when you add a new character or rewrite the lore — so nothing here locks you in.

10 / BUILD ORDER

Every step ends in something playable

Don’t build the whole backend before the first trade feels good. Chase the fun early.

1

The first trade

GOAL → open it, buy a coin, feel the dopamine

Accounts, a server-side wallet, one coin on a bonding curve you can buy and sell. One character to start. Prove the loop is fun before anything else.

2

Create + a living market

GOAL → mint your own meme, watch it move

Coin creation, the tick worker + live prices, mining/passive income. Now both playstyles exist: trade or create.

3

The feed + flex/larp

GOAL → clout becomes real

The social feed, PnL posting with verification, the flex-vs-larp system, clout rating that moves markets and decays. This is your signature — get it right.

4

Characters + story + NPCs

GOAL → it has a soul

The full character roster and come-up stories, the mission engine, and Claude-powered NPCs with memory. Ship the world pack.

5

Polish, mobile, launch

GOAL → shareable and sticky

Fill the desktop apps, go responsive/touch, sound, onboarding, leaderboards, anti-cheat pass. Then tie in the token.
